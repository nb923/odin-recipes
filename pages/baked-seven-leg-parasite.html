Baked Seven Leg Parasite Recipe

Ingredients

  • Potato with Breathing Hole (parasite has to have 7 legs)
  • Olive or Vegetable Oil
  • Chili Flakes

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees
  2. Peel skin until finding the parasite inside
  3. Pull the parasite outside and leave it until it dies
  4. Sprinkle chili flakes on it
  5. Spread oil on a baking tray
  6. Bake for 40 minutes
