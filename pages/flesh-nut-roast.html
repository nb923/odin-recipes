Flesh Nut Roast

Ingredients

  • Flesh Nut
  • Olive or Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees
  2. Pull open nut's lips to reveal teeth and then use a fork to pry it open
  3. Cut all the tendrils with scissors and open the jaw
  4. Take out the inner part of the nut (seed)
  5. Pour watered down ketchup into the feeding hole of the seed to neutralize the poison
  6. Cut off the flesh of the seed and get the meat
  7. Spread oil on a baking tray
  8. Season the meat with salt and black pepper
  9. Put it in the oven for a hour and a half (if it is done it won't scream when poked)
